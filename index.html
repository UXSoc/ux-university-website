Hi, we are User Experience Society!
UXSoc is a student-run organization based in Ateneo de Manila University that was started in 2015. We advocate and practice human-centered design in solving problems in the community. We aim to nurture designers who are passionate about solving problems in the community and want to empower them through design education by encouraging them to create meaningful products and services for the community.

ABOUT UX UNI_

[LEARN, COMPETE, AND INNOVATE]


Started in 2017, UX University is a design challenge to evangelize design through a series of talks, workshops, and webinars. The event walks participants through the process of conducting human-centered design while taking part in a design challenge to equip the participants with the right skills, tools, and mindset to solve pressing issues. Several mentors will be present during the workshop to help and guide the participants with their work.

The theme for this year's case study is Shifting Tides with AI and Design

The emergence of AI has begun and will continue to transform the way we approach user experience. Having enhanced efficiency, accessibility, and personalization in digital products, AI-driven tools have enabled designers to create more intuitive and flexible solutions. However, this rapid integration also creates new ethical challenges, bringing about the need for responsible AI usage.

Recognizing both the opportunities and threats posed by AI, UX University 2025 encourages individuals to explore how these tools can improve human-centered design and empower designers to create meaningful, community-driven products – challenging participants to think critically about how AI and UX design can coexist harmoniously.

FAQs_

Who can join UX University 2025?
The competition is open to anyone passionate in UX Design, AI, and solving problems through human-centered design.
How do I Register?
Registration opens on February 17, 2025 (Monday), and closes on March 7, 2025 (Friday). You may register through [INSERT LINK HERE]
Is there a registration fee?
Yes, the price is as follows:

• Early bird for non-members: PHP 270.00
• Early bird for members: PHP 300.00
• Regular fee for non-members: PHP 420.00
• Regular fee for members: PHP 350.00

Mode of payment will be through Gcash only.
Can I join as an individual or a team?
UX University is an individual competition.
Do I need prior UX Experience to join?
No, the event is open to all skill levels! The workshops and mentorship will guide participants through the entire process.
What will happen during the 6 week program?
Participants will attend talks and workshops on UX and AI and receive mentorship from industry professionals. They will also take on a design challenge aligned with the theme: "Shifting Tides with AI and Design". The top five participants will advance to the final pitch, where they will present their projects to a panel of judges. The top three will be awarded cash prizes.
Do I have to attend all 6 weeks?
Attendance is highly encouraged but not mandatory. Attending these sessions will give you the best learning experience and improve your chances in the competition.
How will the competition be judged?
The judging criteria focuses on two things: Analysis and Execution

• Analysis: AI tools, Understanding, and Problem-Solving
• Execution: Information Architecture and Navigation, Engagement, and Visual Appeal
Will participants receive certificates?
Yes, all participants who complete the program will receive an e-certificate.
